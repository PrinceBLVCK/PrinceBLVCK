Varsity Guide

Description:

Matriculants are confronted with challenges of taking a first step to transition to varsity experience. One of the first steps to be taken is deciding which Career path you want to take, if your results qualify you to take the course that you are passionate about. Varsity Guide is an Android Application that is aimed to address such challenges that Matriculants encounter after having to complete their school years. It provide assistance in calculating Admission Score point( APS), based of their scored points the application will sugguest a list of course in different Universities. The user can click on any course on the list to view more information about the course. When they are interested on the course they can click on the Apply button which will redirect them to the application site of that university.

Technologies:

  • Android SDK
  • Java
  • Google Firebase
    1. Realtime Database
    2. Authentication
